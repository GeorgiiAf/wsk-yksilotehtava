About Opiskelijaravintolat

Opiskelijaravintolat is a web application designed to help students find and explore nearby student restaurants. The platform provides detailed information about restaurants, including their menus, locations, and contact details.

With Opiskelijaravintolat, you can:

  • Browse a list of student restaurants in your area.
  • View daily and weekly menus for each restaurant.
  • Filter restaurants by city or provider to find the best options for you.
  • Save your favorite restaurant for quick access.
  • Navigate to restaurant locations using an interactive map.

Our goal is to make it easier for students to find affordable and convenient dining options while focusing on their studies.
